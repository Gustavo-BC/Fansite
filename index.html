The Legend of Korra

Down below you will see some cards consisting some characters from the avatar world. This group calls themselves, Team Avatar.

Avatar Korra

Korra is the current Avatar and she's finally getting the chance to make her debut.

Mako

Mako is a firebender and brother to Bolin. He's a pro-bender and a good friend to Korra.

Bolin

Bolin, brother to Mako, is a pro-bender who bends earth and labels himself as the funny guy in Team Avatar.

Asami Sato

Asami Sato, a rich girl who owns Future Industries, is smart, beautiful, and an expert in hand to hand combat.
